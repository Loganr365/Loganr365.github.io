The Spooking is a horror exploration game created in unity. I worked in a team for this project as a level designer and was in charge of the audio.

I helped create and flesh out environments and scripted environmental puzzles while also finding, editing, and creating audio clips and the code driving the playback of the game audio in the world. I also created basic 3D objects in Maya to be used as assets in the game and aided in the scripting of player interaction with world objects.

More specifically the player's ability to pick up objects, interact with in-game props based on a key press and ray cast from the players perspective, and sudden unsettling audio and visual effects triggered by player location within the virtual world.
