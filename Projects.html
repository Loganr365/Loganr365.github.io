Here you will find a few of the projects I have worked on.

Audio Visualizer

An audio visualzer programmed enterially in javascript, runs nativly in a user's browser. It allows the user to not only listen to the audio, but also to manipulte the audio and the visualization itself

Pack It Up

A lighthearted physics game created by me and a team of three others in the unity game engine. I served as the lead game designer and oversaw that my original game concept became reality.

The Spookin

The Spooking is a horror exploration game created in unity. I worked in a team for this project as a level designer and was in charge of the audio.

Crowdsassins

Crowdsassins is a multiplayer game created in unity. Players must find each other in large crowded areas by cheating off of each others screens. The first player to find and knock out the other one wins. Be warned, if a player attacks an innocent they will be justly punished.
